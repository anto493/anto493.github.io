Get Icon

Get Icon is an innovative resource that offers knowledge and tools to designers to make their own workflows easier, providing elements that allow them to overcome obstacles in the design phase, in order to achieve results quickly and fun.

Melodya

Melodya is an application born to give a new look to the standard music player. So softly, minimal and colorful at the same time. You will be firstly interfaced with an overview which shows the most popular songs of the moment. You can create your personal playlists with your favourite songs, join them thanks to the lyrics and share them on socials.

L.I.F.E.

LIFE (Live an Intelligent Food Experience) combines multiple elements of a traditional kitchen in a single storage unit: gathered in it, you will find fridge, freezer, pantry, and a vegetable garden. Its external semi-matt frosted glass conceals within itself an interactive surface, a touchscreen display that allows you to dialogue with LIFE and monitor its functions.
